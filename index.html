What is it?

DragonWarriorJS is a recreation of Dragon Warrior for the NES in JavaScript. It aims to be as authentic as possible, warts and all. The feature set currently includes:

  • Smooth movement
  • Towns
  • NPCs capable of complex conversations
  • Buying goods from shops
  • Staying at inns
  • Battles with monsters (though still a little rough here)
